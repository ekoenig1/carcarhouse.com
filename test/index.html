Getting to Camiguin Souldivers

Please be aware that timetables will change so check with the carriers beforehand.

FROM CEBU

Daily flights direct to Camiguin Island by Cebu Pacific Air and Philippine Airlines.

FROM MANILA (NEW)

SkyJet Airlines flies direct from Manila to Camiguin Island for only 80 minutes.

FROM CEBU VIA BOHOL

Via Bohol Island by fast ferry from Cebu to Tagbilaran (Bohol) with OceanJet or 2GO Travel. Then take van or bus to Jagna (approx. 1 hour) and then take the ferry to Camiguin with Super Shuttle Ferry (daily 1:00 pm; approx. 3 hours).

FROM CAGAYAN DE ORO

Take a bus from Agora Bus Terminal to Balingoan (approx. 2.5 hours) and then a ferry to Camiguin (approx. 1 hour). You arrive in Benoni Port, then take a van or a Jeeepney to Mambajao and let the driver drop you off at Camiguin Souldivers.

FROM CAMIGUIN AIRPORT

The Airport is 1 km to the west of the main town Mambajao. We are 7 km south east of Mambajao. Get a multicab taxi (approx. â‚± 200), tricycle (approx. â‚± 150) or motorbike taxi (approx. â‚± 100).
